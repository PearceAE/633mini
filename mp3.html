Mini Project 3

For Mini Project 3 I choose to complete the audio I will use for my ePortfolio page. I took two audio files, Beethoven's Moonlight Sonata and Scherzo. I trimmed both the tracks down using Audacity. I then slowed down the tempo of the second track, Scherzo before merging the tracks together. I added a fade-in and fade-out on both tracks after they were trimmed for better transition. Lastly, I added my voiceover. For my voice audio, I adjusted the pitch and speed slightly. Once my voice audio was combined, I adjusted the gain -11 on the background music and then adjusted the amplification on Schrezo track to -0.2.
